Ada Lovelace

The short life story of Ada

  • 1815: Born in London, England
  • 1833: Introduced to Charles Babbage
  • 1835: Married William, 8th Baron King
  • 1842-43: Translated the Italian mathematician Luigi Menabrea's article on Babbage's newest proposed machine, the Analytical Engine.
  • 1842: Wrote Note G with complete detail a method for calculating a sequence of Bernoulli numbers using the Analytical Engine. Considered to be the first computer program.
  • 1852: Ada Lovelace died of uterine cancer.
  • 1980:U.S. Department of Defense settled on the name "Ada" for a new standardized computer language, named in honor of Ada Lovelace.
